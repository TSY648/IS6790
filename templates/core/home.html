About This Game

This prototype teaches data literacy through supermarket management scenarios. Players move from intuition-led choices to evidence-based decision making across a structured level journey.

  • Inspect evidence from NPC dialogue, reports, and external information.
  • Compare signals against noise before making a business decision.
  • Use each result screen to reflect on why a decision succeeded or failed.
